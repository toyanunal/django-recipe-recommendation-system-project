FAQs



Do I need a special pot for recipes?

You do not need special equipment; you can make our recipes with your home pot unless specifically indicated in the recipe.

Can I double the recipe?

You can double the recipe by doubling the ingredients.

I don’t want to use oil; what do you suggest using to substitute oil in your recipes?

It depends on the recipe. You can use different things instead of oil, but you may not get the desired result in taste.

Can I share your recipes on other sites?

You can share as long as you give references.

Can we upload our own recipes to your site?

We do not have such a feature for now, but we are planning.

Can I find recipes from world cuisine?

Yes, you can. We bring you together with all the world’s cuisine according to your materials. We also have a goal of doubling our recipes every month.

Can I use margarine in place of butter?

Probably not. It’s too watery and doesn’t behave like butter. We suggest vegan butter such as Earth Balance sticks rather than margarine. Still, butter is all we bake with when it is needed in our recipes.

Do you offer vegan recipes?

Yes, we do. All recipes are tagged according to the diet type. The recipes tagged as vegan contains only vegan ingredients.

How often are the unit prices of the ingredients updated?

Ingredients’ unit costs are updated on the first day of every month.

Where do you get these unit prices for the ingredients?

Our ingredient prices are taken from the website of our supplier company based on the units of kilogram, liter, and piece with reference to the retail sales prices.
